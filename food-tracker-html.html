Creamy Chicken and Rice Soup

This soup is a comforting, hearty meal that is perfect for warming up on a cold winter day. It's made with tender chicken, creamy rice, and a rich, flavorful broth. Serve it with some crusty bread for a complete meal. This recipe is easy to make and can be made in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients

2 cups cooked chicken, shredded or chopped

Instructions

1
In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the onion, carrots, and celery, and cook for 5-7 minutes, until the vegetables are soft.
2
Add the garlic and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
3
Stir in the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes, until the mixture is golden.
